Goulash Recipe

Why Goulash?

Goulash has always been one of my favorite meals. Not only is it easy to make but its profile matches all of the criteria one would say is desired. It's savory beef and melted cheese combine to make the perfect texture.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cheese (any kind you'd like)
  • Noodles
  • Various Spices
  • Diced Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Season the Meat
  3. Boil Water and Add Noodles
  4. Drain Fully Cooked Noodles and Put Back in Pot
  5. Add Meat to Pot Full of Cooked Noodles
  6. Add Sauce and Cheese to Same Pot
  7. Mix Everything Together
  8. ENJOY!
