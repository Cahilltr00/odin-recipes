Cereal Recipe

Why Cereal?

Cereal has got to be one of the all time classics for an American breakfast. I chose it also because it's a simple recipe that even kid can understand. Just beware of the sugar traps.

Ingredients

  • One Bowl
  • One Spoon
  • Your Favorite Kind of Cereal
  • One Good Pour of Nice Cold Milk

Steps

  1. Pour a Good Amount of Cereal Into the Bowl
  2. Fill Bowl up 1/3 the Way With Milk
  3. Place Spoon in Bowl
  4. Scoop One Mouthful into Mouth
  5. ENJOY!
